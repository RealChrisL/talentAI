02 / 14 · The Problem

Professional firms lose paying cases to silence.

  • License-gated firms (代書, clinics, agencies) run intake on a personal LINE account.
  • Nearly a third of clients first message when no human is at the desk.
  • Before the agent, those messages met silence until the next morning.
  • In an anxiety-driven, referral economy, the client who waits messages the next firm.
  • A 1–3 person office cannot staff 24/7 shift cover at any wage it can pay.

Measured: ~30% of clients first contacted 全謹代書 outside Mon–Fri 09:00–18:00 (195 of 641). The pain is the third of demand that hits a dead channel.

03 / 14 · The Solution

Agentic intake that works inside the rules, then steps aside.

  • We embed a compliance-guarded AI operations layer into the firm's existing channel (LINE OA).
  • It qualifies leads, runs the full business-specific questionnaire, and builds the CRM record in real time.
  • The licensed professional owns every judgment, fee, and close — the agent does intake and triage only.
  • High-value cases auto-escalate to the responsible person with full conversation context attached.
  • Answers in seconds, 24/7; the licensee acts when they choose, on a pre-documented case.

Human-in-the-loop is structural, not a promise — the code enforces it (status flips to 人工接管中 and the agent goes silent).

09 / 14 · Security & Trust

Isolated compute, no-train AI, full audit trail.
